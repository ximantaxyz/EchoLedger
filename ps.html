Respectful Use

EchoLedger is a public, transparent project built to acknowledge followers. Respect for individuals—both listed and unlisted—is mandatory.

Harassment, threats, doxxing, abuse, or any form of harmful behavior directed at people in the ledger or its maintainers is strictly prohibited.

This project values human dignity above all. Use the ledger responsibly and with maturity.

No Misuse or Manipulation

The ledger is read‑only and presented as a static archive. You may not:

  • Scrape, crawl, or automate extraction of ledger data
  • Impersonate or misrepresent affiliation with EchoLedger
  • Alter, fake, forge, or attempt to manipulate records
  • Use ledger data for commercial, promotional, or spam purposes
  • Reverse‑engineer or attempt to exploit the project’s structure

The integrity of the ledger is protected; violations may result in access restrictions.

Reporting Concerns

If you believe content is incorrect, outdated, or presented in a harmful manner, please contact us. We review all reports manually and with care.

You may report:

  • Inaccurate or unauthorized entries
  • Safety or harassment concerns
  • Technical vulnerabilities
  • Policy or moderation questions

Use the Contact button above or email directly at ximanta.official@gmail.com. We aim to respond within a reasonable timeframe.

Community Expectations

This project operates on principles of transparency, respect, and good faith. Users who engage with the ledger—whether viewing, sharing, or referencing it—are expected to act maturely and responsibly.

Bad‑faith behavior, deliberate disruption, or attempts to undermine the project’s purpose may result in permanent restrictions from accessing EchoLedger resources.

Our goal is to maintain a safe, stable, and trustworthy archive. Your cooperation is essential.

Changes & Enforcement

These safety rules may evolve as the project grows or as new challenges emerge. Changes will be reflected on this page with clear communication.

Enforcement decisions are made at the sole discretion of the project maintainers, with the intent of protection—not punishment. All decisions are final.

By using EchoLedger, you agree to abide by these safety guidelines and acknowledge that violation may lead to access restrictions without prior notice.
